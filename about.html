About Us

MyHotelFinder is a hotel discovery platform proudly built by students as part of a real-world college project.

Whether you're seeking luxury in Makati or budget-friendly options in Cebu, we help you explore and compare stays across the Philippines — all with just a few clicks.

Our Mission

To empower travelers with smart, fast, and reliable tools to find the perfect hotel. We aim to make travel planning easier for students, families, and professionals alike.

\ Built by Students

This project showcases how design, development, and passion come together in a learning environment. Every feature you see — from hotel search to responsive design — was coded by us from scratch as part of a semester-long journey.

What Makes Us Exciting

  • Clean, student-friendly design
  • Fully responsive layout (try resizing!)
  • Inspired by real hotel booking platforms
  • 100% built for learning and creativity

Thank you for visiting MyHotelFinder. We hope you enjoy exploring as much as we enjoyed building it!
